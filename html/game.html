Swordy the Sword

Fight through rooms, time your parries, and spend your flakes at the blacksmith.

Controls: use the on-screen Attack and Parry buttons. Parries are limited each room, so use them carefully.

Fiddly details:
Critical hits are x2 damage (yellow number)
Regenerate x1 parry by landing a hit
Upgrades:
(1) Damage: Increases Swordy's attack damage.
(2) Speed: Decreases Swordy's attack cooldown.
(3) Parry: Increases Swordy's parry count.
(4) Health: Increases Swordy's hitpoints.
