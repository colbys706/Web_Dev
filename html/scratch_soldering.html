Tips for Desoldering

  • Set-up
    1. Set solder at 650-750 deg F
    2. Mix 63/37 solder with old solder
    3. Once solder is mixed, it will be easier to melt and remove
  • Option 1: Wick and Flux
    1. Cut 1in. wick/soldering braid
    2. Apply flux to joint and wick
    3. Using tweasers, hold wick to joint
    4. Press solder on top of wick
    5. Wait till solder stops soaking in
    6. Use isopropyl alcohol to clean used flux
    7. Repeat as needed
  • Desoldering Pump
    1. Pre-load desoldering pump
    2. Apply soldering tip to joint
    3. With solder still molten, place pump tip over joint
    4. (its tip is heat resistant)
    5. Activate pump-->pump a couple more times till solder is gone
    6. Repeat as needed
